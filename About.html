Unidentified Flying Object (UFO)

An unidentified flying object or "UFO" is an object perceived in the sky that is not readily identified. Most UFOs are later identified as conventional objects or phenomena. The term is widely used for claimed observations of extraterrestrial craft.

This study is based on some of the sightings from Australia, Canada, United Kingdom and United States between 2010 and 2014. A total of 23,820 sightings were taken into consideration for this study during this timeline. A majority of the sightings were noticed in United States(22,991). The shapes of the UFOs are also analyzed, with a majority of sightings in the shape of light(5,253).


The analysis of the sightings in United States by state show that the sightings vary by state with California having the highest number of sightings (2,687), followed by Florida(1,574) and Washington(1,278). There seems to be no pattern on the sightings.
